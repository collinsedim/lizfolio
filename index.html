About Me

Hi there! My name is Collins Edim and I am a web developer, copywriter, and SEO specialist with over 5 years of experience in the industry.

I have a strong foundation in web development with a wide range of skills including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, GIT, React JS, NodeJS, SQL, and more. Additionally, I have a wealth of experience in SEO and web performance optimization, helping to ensure that websites are optimized for search engines and providing the best possible user experience.

My skills also extend to responsive design, mobile-first design, and using tools such as Figma, WordPress, and Shopify to develop websites that are both visually stunning and highly functional. I am constantly learning new skills and keeping up to date with the latest trends in web development to ensure that I am always providing my clients with the most innovative and effective solutions.

If you are looking for a skilled and experienced web developer, copywriter, or SEO specialist, then look no further! I am passionate about what I do and am dedicated to providing my clients with the highest level of service and expertise.
